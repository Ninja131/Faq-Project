Frequently asked questions

Why did you choose UI/UX as a career?

Because apparently, staring at buttons for 6 hours and arguing over font sizes is a real job now.

How do you stay updated in frontend development?

I don’t. I just keep adding display: flex until things look fine and hope nobody asks questions.

Why does every project take longer than expected?

Because I spend the first 2 hours choosing the perfect shade of pastel and the next 3 crying over a broken navbar.

What’s the most challenging part of being a frontend dev?

Explaining to my family that “yes, I’m working” while I’m just resizing icons for the 10th time.

How do you handle feedback like ‘make it more modern’?

I nod, add a shadow, change one font weight, and act like I just reinvented the wheel.
